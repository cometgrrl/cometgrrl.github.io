Welcome to Quality Boss

Welcome to Quality Boss, a blog where quality engineering, management, leadership, and the experiences of being a woman in tech intersect. I'm Brienna, and I wear multiple hats in the tech industry. As my main job, I lead a dynamic team of quality engineers, ensuring that our products meet the highest standards of quality and reliability.

But my journey in tech goes beyond that. In addition to my role as a quality engineering lead, I have also had the privilege of leading The Women of Automattic, an Employee Resource Group. These experiences allow me to use my unique perspective to help understand and address the challenges and opportunities that women face in the tech world.

What You'll Find Here

Quality Boss is a space where I share insights, reflections, and musings on a wide range of topics:

  • Quality Engineering: Dive deep into the world of quality engineering, where I'll share best practices, tools, and strategies to ensure that software and products meet and exceed user expectations.
  • Leadership & Management: Explore the art and science of leadership and management. From team dynamics to effective communication, I'll discuss the skills and strategies that help me lead my team to success.
  • Being a Woman in Tech: As a woman in a male-dominated industry, I understand the unique challenges and triumphs that come with it. I'll share personal stories, tips, and advice for navigating and thriving in the tech world.
  • Personal Reflections: My personal thoughts on spanning or going beyond the other categories.

Join the Conversation

Quality Boss is not just about my experiences; it's a platform for dialogue and connection. I encourage you to join the conversation by leaving comments, sharing your own insights, and reaching out to me. Together, we can create a supportive and empowering community for quality engineers, leaders, and women in tech.

Thank you for visiting Quality Boss. I hope you find inspiration, information, and camaraderie here. Let's embark on this journey of continuous improvement and empowerment together.

- Brienna
